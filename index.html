About Our Mobile-Only Detailing Service

At The Auto Refinery, we believe every vehicle deserves the highest level of care - delivered right to your doorstep. As a mobile-only detailing service, we bring premium auto detailing directly to homes and businesses throughout Modesto and Central Valley, eliminating the need to visit a traditional shop.

The Auto Refinery was founded by Joseph Dunlap, a 19-year-old college student with a passion for mobile car detailing. With 4 years of experience providing at-your-location detailing services, he handles all aspects of this mobile-only business. From website design to making tires shine in your driveway, his passion and dedication is seen in the showroom-quality mobile details delivered right where you park.

Frequently Asked Questions

How do I book a service?

Click the "Book Now" button anywhere on the site or visit our Booking Page to schedule your appointment. You can also call us directly at (209)-214-9029.

What products do you use?

We use only premium, industry-leading products for all detailing and protection services. Our ceramic coatings are professional-grade with 3-5 year durability, and our cleaning products are pH-balanced and safe for all surfaces.

How long does a detail take?

Service times vary: Silver Detail (1-2 hours), Gold Detail (2-3 hours), Platinum Detail (2-4 hours), Paint Correction (4-6 hours), Ceramic Coating (1-3 days including prep work).
