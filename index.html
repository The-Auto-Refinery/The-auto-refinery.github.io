About The Auto Refinery

Frequently Asked Questions

How do I book a service?

Click the "Book Now" button anywhere on the site or visit our Booking Page to schedule your appointment. You can also call us directly at (209)-214-9029.

What products do you use?

We use only premium, industry-leading products for all detailing and protection services. Our ceramic coatings are professional-grade with 3-5 year durability, and our cleaning products are pH-balanced and safe for all surfaces.

How long does a detail take?

Service times vary: Silver Detail (1-2 hours), Gold Detail (2-3 hours), Platinum Detail (2-4 hours), Paint Correction (4-6 hours), Ceramic Coating (1-3 days including prep work).
