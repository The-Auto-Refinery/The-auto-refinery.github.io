The Process

This job included an exterior detail of a 2001 Lexus. The customer heard about our $100 exterior detail offer and couldn't pass it up! The client was thrilled with the results, noting the flawless finish and attention to detail. Our professional detailing services ensure that every vehicle we work on looks its best, inside and out.

For this job, we started with a thorough exterior wash to remove dirt and grime. We then rinsed and did a second wash to catch anything the first one may have missed and to better prep the surface for wax. After rinsing and drying, a high-quality wax was applied to protect the finish. The wheels and tires were cleaned and dressed, and all exterior trim was treated to maximize it's appearance.

Even though this was only an exterior job, the customer had mentioned wanting to clean his floor mats. At the Auto Refinery, we pride ourselves on going above and beyond so at no cost to the cusotmer we shampooed and extracted his floor mats, which no doubt added to the finished product. Safe to say this was an amazing detail and a satisfied customer.
