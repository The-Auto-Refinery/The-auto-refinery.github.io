SHINE SHINE SHINE

Family Hauler Turned Clean Cruiser

The Process

This job is actually split into two parts, in this post we will cover the first appointment to do the exterior. This job was one of our biggest vehicles yet, but that didnt stop us. The customer wanted us to breathe life into the paint and transform this from a simple family hauler to a showroom shocker.

Initially, the vehicle had a lot of old dirt and grime that needed to be removed, so we started with a pre rinse and soak to really uplift the dirt before the wash. We then rinsed and washed the vehicle a second time, before dryinga and applying wax to protect the paint and keep it shining.

In terms of finishing touches, the rims and tires were given a thourough clean and dressing to ensure we left no stone unturned. The end result? This car looks just as good as the day it left the factory and one more happy customer.
