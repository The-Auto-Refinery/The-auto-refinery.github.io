Black Cars can Look Clean Too!

Black Beauty

This job involved a complete exterior and interior detailing for a luxury sedan. The client was thrilled with the results, noting the flawless finish and attention to detail. Our professional detailing services ensure that every vehicle we work on looks its best, inside and out.

The Process

For this job, we started with a thorough exterior wash to remove dirt and grime. We then applied a clay bar treatment to eliminate any embedded contaminants. The paint was polished to restore its shine, and a high-quality wax was applied to protect the finish. The wheels and tires were cleaned and dressed, and all exterior trim was treated to restore its original appearance.

Inside the vehicle, we performed a deep cleaning of all surfaces. The carpets and seats were shampooed, and the upholstery was treated to remove stains and odors. The dashboard, console, and door panels were cleaned and conditioned, and the windows were polished to a streak-free shine. Our attention to detail ensures that every part of the interior looks and feels like new.
