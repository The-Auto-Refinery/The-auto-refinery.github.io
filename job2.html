Black Cars can Look Clean Too!

Black Beauty

The Process

This job involved a complete exterior and interior detailing for a luxury BMW sedan. Our client needed his car to look like new for his prom night, and we transformed it into the cleanest car there. Our professional soap and wax gave his black pearl paint a beautiful shine.

This job started with a pre rinse and soak, to help agitate the existing dirt and grime. We followed that up with another wash to remove the last of the dirt and contaminants. Once the car was rinsed off, we applied a claybar treatment to the paint before finishing it all off with a high quality wax. This not only protects the paint but gives it an amazing shine that will last long and repel water.

Inside the vehicle, we performed a deep cleaning of all surfaces. The carpets and seats were shampooed, and the upholstery was treated to remove stains and odors. The dashboard, console, and door panels were cleaned and conditioned, and the windows were polished to a streak-free shine. We aim to make your car look and feel like new. This customer asked, and we delivered. Another great detail and one more happy customer.
