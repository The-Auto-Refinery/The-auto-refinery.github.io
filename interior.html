Interior Auto Detailing

Spotless and Fresh

Choose from our interior detailing options to keep your car's interior looking and feeling new. Our services include deep cleaning, stain removal, leather conditioning, and odor elimination. Experience the best interior auto detailing in Modesto, CA with The Auto Refinery. Our prices are determined by the size of the vehicle and the nature of the request. Please call for a free quote!

Our Basic Interior Cleaning service includes vacuuming of carpets and seats, dusting of surfaces, and cleaning of windows. This service is perfect for maintaining a clean and tidy interior on a regular basis. Keep your car's interior fresh and inviting with our Basic Interior Cleaning.

Our Deep Interior Cleaning service goes beyond the basics to provide a thorough cleaning of your car's interior. This includes shampooing of carpets and seats, deep cleaning of upholstery, and detailed cleaning of all surfaces. We also treat and condition leather seats to keep them soft and supple. Choose our Deep Interior Cleaning for a comprehensive interior refresh.

Our Leather Treatment service includes cleaning, conditioning, and protecting your car's leather surfaces. We use high-quality products to remove dirt and stains, restore the leather's natural shine, and protect it from future damage. Keep your leather seats looking and feeling luxurious with our Leather Treatment service.
